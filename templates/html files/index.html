Bapuji Premier League

Every year, CSE department of BIET organises a sports tournament named as Bapuji Premier League (BPL). Students from various branches actively participate in this event in teams.

Cricket is the major sport organised and Badminton is newly added for the tournament. All the branches of BIET encourage their students to participate and represent their respective branch. The cricket is dedicatedly organised for boys and Badminton for girl students.
